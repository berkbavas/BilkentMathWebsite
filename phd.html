Ph.D. Doctoral Degree Program

Doctoral Degree Program

Designed to cultivate independent researchers with originality and creativity, and to prepare students for a professional research career.

Apply Online Curriculum Graduate Program Overview

Research-first

Thesis-driven program with emphasis on original work and independent investigation.

Advisor-guided

Each program is individually planned with a faculty advisor.

Typical duration

8 semesters (above M.S.) or 10 semesters (above B.S.), extendable for 4 more.

Purpose

A doctoral degree program is intended to enhance originality and creativity in research and teaching. It is expected to lead students to independent investigation of a problem in their fields, so that they can make a significant contribution to the existing body of knowledge.

Although a part of the task is the acquisition of existing knowledge in the field, the fundamental objective is to develop the skill and capacity to conduct original research in the generation of new knowledge.

The preliminary requirement for the doctorate is regarded not as an end in itself, but as preparation for the dissertation in which the candidate demonstrates this ability.

Admission

Applicants must have an M.S. or B.S. degree in Mathematics (or in relevant areas of science or engineering). Evaluation is based on ALES, academic record, and interview. Applicants unable to take ALES are evaluated by GRE. Satisfactory English proficiency is required.

Please refer to the Graduate School of Engineering and Science requirements: mfbe.bilkent.edu.tr

Degree requirements

In addition to course work, the Ph.D. student is expected to pass a qualifying examination, and to prepare and defend a dissertation based on original research.

  • Course work: at least 21 credits above M.S. level (or 42 above B.S. level)
  • Qualifying exam: successful completion required
  • Dissertation: original research, defense required
