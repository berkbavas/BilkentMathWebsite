Welcome to Bilkent Mathematics

Bilkent Matematik Bölümü'ne Hoş Geldiniz

The Department of Mathematics at Bilkent University offers undergraduate and graduate courses that lead to B.S., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Mathematics, as well as undergraduate and graduate courses for all departments of the university.

Bilkent Matematik Bölümü, matematikte lisans, yüksek lisans ve doktoraderecelerine yönelik eğitimin yanısıra, üniversitenin tümbölümleri için de lisans ve lisansüstü seviyelerinde matematik dersleri verir.

The department emphasizes both pure and applied mathematics. Research in the department covers algebra, algebraic topology, algebraic geometry, complex analysis, functional analysis, number theory, differential equations and dynamical systems, mathematical physics, and applied mathematics.
