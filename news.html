2014

Former faculty member Prof. Dr. Cem Yalçın Yıldırım, together with Daniel Goldston, János Pintz, and Yitang Zhang, received the 2014 Cole Prize in Number Theory for their work on small gaps between primes.

The group also delivered an invited talk at the International Congress of Mathematicians (ICM 2014) , held in Seoul, South Korea.

The department hosted the 6th Computational Methods and Function Theory (CMFT 2009) conference in June 2009.
